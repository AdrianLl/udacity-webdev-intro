Immortal Jellyfish

Turritopsis dohrnii, the immortal jellyfish, is a species of small, biologically immortal jellyfish found in the Mediterranean Sea and in the waters of Japan.

  • Scientific Name: Turritopsis dohrnii
  • Average Length: Diameter 4.5 mm
  • Average Lifespan: Biologically Immortal
  • Habitat: Mediterranean Sea

The medusa of Turritopsis dohrnii is bell-shaped, with a maximum diameter of about 4.5 millimetres (0.18 in) and is about as tall as it is wide.
